- Import and apply globally

  - line-height: very tight (leading-[0.9])
  - letter-spacing: slightly tight (-tracking-wide)

- Subtitle:
  - font-weight: 400
  - font-size: text-xl or text-2xl
  - color: gray-400

- Top small text:
  - font-size: text-sm
  - uppercase
  - letter-spacing: tracking-widest
  - color: gray-500

- Full screen height (min-h-screen)
- Center everything vertically and horizontally
- Max width container (max-w-5xl mx-auto)

- Minimal, no extra colors
